Workflow and Structure

Customer Portal

  1. Customer uploads shopping/grocery/market list with cost for each item needed and cost total.
  2. Inputs location of delivery of shopping.

 

System Admin Portal

 

 

  1. Receives shopping/grocery/market list.
  2. Estimates service charge, delivery cost & sends a response back to customer portal.

Customer Portal

  1. Customer receives response and makes payment either with transfer or ATM card through the payment gateway enabled on the platform.

 

Dispatcher Portal

 

 

  1. Dispatcher portal determines the most convenient/closest shopper to request/area or shuffles and selects based set conditions.

Shopper Portal

 

 

  1. Shopper indicates availability either by coming online(on the app)/being online/ or not already allocated on a shopping task
  2. Shopper receives shopping request as allocated by Dispatch portal
  3. Shopper sets request and confirms the shopping task. Customer is notified and given an estimated time of arrival(ETA) for the delivery of items on the list. Maximum estimated time is 12 hours(same day delivery)

 

System Admin Portal

 

 

  1. System relays to customer's portal that a Shopper has been assigned, and the shopper's location is made known to the customer with ETA to Customer's location.

Customer Portal

  1. Customer receives system information on Shopper and his/her location at the time of the request with the estimated time of delivery of items.

 

Shopper Portal

 

 

  1. Shopper confirms start of shopping trip upon receiving list.
  2. Shopper confirms delivery of items upon getting to customer's location

 

Features

Customer App

  • Set delivery of shopping location by map
  • Upload shopping/grocery/market list
  • Show shoppers' locations on map in Realtime
  • Book A Scheduled Shopping
  • Save Favourite delivery Locations
  • Apply Promo codes
  • Choose Payment Method as Cash/Card/Wallet
  • Add Money to Wallet by ATM Card
  • Localization Supports
  • Multiple Currency Support
  • SOS-Realtime Notifications to system administrator
  • Referral Program
  • In-App chat B/W Shopper & Customer
  • Ratings & Review

 

Shopper App

 

 

  • Switch Into shifts(Available/Not Available)
  • Manage Documents & Approval
  • Earnings Dashboard
  • Realtime location update during shopping
  • Accept shopping with Otp Feature
  • Add Money to Wallet by ATM Card
  • Localization Supports
  • SOS-Realtime Notifications to System administrator
  • Referral Program
  • In-App chat B/W Shopper & Customer

System Admin Portal

  • Price configuration for service charge on shop now/ Schedule shopping

  • SOS Number configuration
  • Promo code configuration
